How It Works

A simple process for customers and vendors

1. Purchase with Reusable Container

Customers receive their food in QR-coded containers linked to their account or transaction.

2. Scan QR Code When Returning

Vendors scan the QR code to register the return and track container status in real-time.

3. Earn Rewards & Repeat

Customers receive discounts or loyalty points while vendors save on packaging costs.
